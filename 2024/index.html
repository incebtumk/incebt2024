   ABOUT EVENT

The 12th International Conference on Entrepreneurship, Business & Technology (InCEBT 2024) is organized by through the Faculty of Entrepreneurship and Business (FEB), Universiti Malaysia Kelantan (UMK), and it will be conducted on the 12th and 13th of September 2024 via physical at Universiti Malaysia Kelantan, City Campus and through the virtual mode in pre-recorded video  presentation. This year, InCEBT 2024 is part of The World Entrepreneurship, Science and Technology Summit 2024 (WESTS2024).

The InCEBT 2024 aims to facilitate knowledge exchange between academic, researchers and professionals, allowing them to share experiences and research findings in the broad fields of entrepreneurship, business and technology. 

The InCEBT 2024 accepts 
           - Full research papers
           - Conceptual papers
           - Review papers 
describing their original works on all aspects of related fields. Participant* are also encouraged to attend for networking and sharing-session opportunities.

ALL accepted and presented papers will be published in either 
        - Scopus Indexed conference proceedings
        - One of the MyCite (Malaysia Citation Index) journals
        - Scopus or Web of Science (WoS) journal(s) if specific criteria are met

An e-CERTIFICATE will be provided to every registered participant.

*A participant or an observer is a person who is not participating to present AND submitting paper(s)
